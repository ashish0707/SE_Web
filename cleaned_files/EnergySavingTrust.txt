energy saving trust est is a british organization devoted to promoting energy efficiency energy conservation and the sustainable use of energy thereby reducing carbon dioxide emissions and helping to prevent man-made climate change it was founded in the united kingdom as a government-sponsored initiative in 1992 following the global earth summit1 energy saving trust is an independent
