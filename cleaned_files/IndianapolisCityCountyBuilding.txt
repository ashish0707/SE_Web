200 e washington street in downtown indianapolis indiana that houses the offices of the consolidated city-county government of indianapolis and marion county indiana known as unigov in its 665,534 square feet 1 of office space it also houses the marion county courts and the headquarters of the indianapolis metropolitan police department and the indianapolis city-county council the office of the
