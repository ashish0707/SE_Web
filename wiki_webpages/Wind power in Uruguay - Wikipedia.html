Wind power in Uruguay

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search
Sierra de los Caracoles wind farm.

Wind power in Uruguay generates a small but rapidly growing proportion of the country's electricity.[1] Projections consider that, at the end of 2016, Uruguay could become the country with the largest energy coverage with wind power.[2]

Wind farms[edit]

There are about 30 wind farms in Uruguay:[3]

  • Peralta wind farm, located in Tacuarembó Department[4]
  • Sierra de los Caracoles.
