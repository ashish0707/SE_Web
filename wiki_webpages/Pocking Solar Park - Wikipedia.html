The Pocking Solar Park is a photovoltaic power station in Pocking, Lower Bavaria, Germany. It has installed capacity of 10 megawatts (MW). Construction and assembly of the power plant started in August 2005 and was completed in March 2006.

The power plant is located on 7.5 hectares (19 acres) on the former military training area. It has 57,912 modules.[1]
