Neuhardenberg Solar Park is a 145-megawatt (MW) photovoltaic power plant, and was Europe's largest solar power station, located at the former Neuhardenberg military airport.[3]

In 2015 a solar battery storage facility with a rated power of 5 MW and 5 MWh capacity was added, which was commissioned in mid 2016.[4]
