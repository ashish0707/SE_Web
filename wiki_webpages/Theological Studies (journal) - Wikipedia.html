Theological Studies is a quarterly peer-reviewed academic journal published by the Society of Jesus in the United States covering research on all aspects of theology. The journal is hosted by Marquette University and was established in 1940.[1] The current editor-in-chief is David G. Schultenover (Marquette University).[1]
