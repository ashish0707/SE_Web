The world's all-time two best selling all-electric cars are the Nissan Leaf (left), with almost 240,000 global sales, and the Tesla Model S (right), with over 145,000 units, both as of September 2016[update].[1][2][3]

This is a list of production electric cars, classified by type of vehicle according to its speed capabilities: low speed or neighborhood electric vehicle, urban or city car, highway-capable and raceway.
