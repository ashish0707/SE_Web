Solar power in Myanmar

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

Solar power in Burma has the potential to generate 51,973.8 TWh/year, with an average of over 5 sun hours per day.

In rural areas, photovoltaics are used for charging batteries and pumping water.[1] 70% of the Burmese population of 50 million live in rural areas.[2]
