Bairnsdale Power Station

Bairnsdale Power Station is a 94MW Natural Gas-fired power station in Victoria’s East Gippsland region. It is a fast start, peaking power station dispatching into the National Electricity Market. Bairnsdale is connected to the SP AusNet distribution network grid.

The station was commissioned in 2001.
