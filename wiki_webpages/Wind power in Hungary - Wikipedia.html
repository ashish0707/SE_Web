Wind power in Hungary

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search
Wind farm near Mosonszolnok

The installed capacity of wind power in Hungary was 329 MW as of April 2011.[1] Most of wind farms are in the Kisalföld region.

As of 1 April 2011, there were 39 operational wind farms in Hungary, with 172 turbines and 329 MW of installed capacity.

Tenders[edit]

  • The first tender was written in 2006 and it contains 330 MW capacity. Till March 16, 2006 it received 1138 MW capacity.[2]
  • In 2009 Hungary tendered for 410 MW of new wind capacity. It received 68 bids totalling 1100 MW capacity,[3] but later the Hungarian Energy Office cancelled it.[4]

Largest wind farms[edit]

  • Kisigmánd Wind Farm - 50 MW
  • Ikervár Wind Farm - 34 MW
  • Ács Wind Farm - 32 MW
  • Bőny Wind Farm - 25 MW
  • Levél Wind Farm - 24 MW
  • Mosonszolnok Wind Farm - 24 MW
