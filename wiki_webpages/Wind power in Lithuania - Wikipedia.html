Wind power in Lithuania

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search
Wind turbine in Lithuania

Wind power in Lithuania is a form of renewable energy in Lithuania. At the end of 2011 wind power capacity in Lithuania was 179 MW and the wind energy share of total electricity consumption was 3,8%.[1]
