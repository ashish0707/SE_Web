Let Us All Unite and Celebrate Together
anthem of African Union
Also known as Unissons-nous tous et célébrons ensemble (French); Unámonos todos y celebremos juntos (Spanish); Vamos todos nos unir e celebrar juntos (Portuguese); فلنتحد جميعا ونحتفل معا `dyuna ntxhd gmiya unxhtfl mya (Arabic); Hebu wote kuungana na kusherehekea pamoja (Swahili)
Music sample
Let Us All Unite and Celebrate Together (Instrumental)

"Let Us All Unite and Celebrate Together" (French: Unissons-nous tous et célébrons ensemble; Spanish: Unámonos todos y celebremos juntos; Portuguese: Vamos todos nos unir e celebrar juntos; Arabic: فلنتحد جميعا ونحتفل معا `dyuna ntxhd gmiya unxhtfl mya‎‎; Swahili: Hebu wote kuungana na kusherehekea pamoja) is the anthem of the African Union.
