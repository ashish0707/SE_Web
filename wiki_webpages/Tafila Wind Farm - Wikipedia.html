Tafila Wind Farm is 117 MW wind farm located in Tafilah Governorate, Jordan. The farm consists of 38 turbines, inaugurated in December 2015 by King Abdullah II. It is considered to be the first of its kind in the Kingdom and the region.[1] The venture aims to diversify energy resources in Jordan and boost the renewable energy contribution in the total energy mix.[2]
