List of countries by coal production

This is a list of countries by coal production in 2014, based mostly on the Statistical Review of World Energy published in 2015 by BP,[1] ranking countries with coal production larger than 10 millions tonnes. Shares are based on data expressed in tonnes oil equivalent.
