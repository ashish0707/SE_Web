Senghor University

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search
Senghor University building

Senghor University (in French: Université Senghor d'Alexandrie) is a university sponsored by the international organization, the Francophonie. Located at Ahmed Orabi Square, Manshiyya, al-, Alexandria, Egypt.
