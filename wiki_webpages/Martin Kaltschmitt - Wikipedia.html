After having studied drilling engineering at Clausthal University of Technology from 1981 to 1986, Kaltschmitt obtained his PhDs at Stuttgart University in 1990, researching the possibilities and limits of wind power and solar energy generation in Baden-W√ºrttemberg. In 2006 he became head of the Institut f√ºr Umwelttechnik und Energiewirtschaft at Technical University of Hamburg.
