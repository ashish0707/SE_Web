Perpignan is the railway station serving the city of Perpignan, Pyrénées-Orientales department, southern France. Part of the station was decorated by Salvador Dalí.

Perpignan is the railway station serving the city of Perpignan, Languedoc-Roussillon, France. The station opened in 1858 and is located on the Narbonne–Portbou railway, LGV Perpignan–Figueres and Perpignan–Villefranche-de-Conflent railway. The station is served by TGV, Intercités and TER services operated by SNCF.


Train services[edit]

The following services currently call at Perpignan:

  • High speed services (TGV) Paris - Nîmes - Montpellier - Perpignan - Barcelona
  • High speed services (TGV) Paris - Nîmes - Montpellier - Perpignan
  • High speed services (AVE) Lyon - Nîmes - Montpellier - Perpignan - Barcelona
  • High speed services (AVE) Toulouse - Carcassonne – Perpignan - Barcelona
  • High speed services (AVE) Marseille - Nîmes - Montpellier - Perpignan - Barcelona - Madrid
  • Intercity services (Intercités) Paris - Limoges - Toulouse - Narbonne - Perpignan - Cerbère
  • Night services (Intercités de nuit) Paris - Limoges - Narbonne - Portbou
  • Night services (Intercités de nuit) Strasbourg/Luxembourg - Nîmes - Narbonne - Portbou
  • Express service (TER Languedoc-Roussillon) Perpignan - Narbonne - Montpellier - Nîmes - Avignon
  • Local service (TER Languedoc-Roussillon) Portbou - Perpignan - Narbonne
  • Local service (TER Languedoc-Roussillon) Villefranche-Vernet-les-Bains - Perpignan
