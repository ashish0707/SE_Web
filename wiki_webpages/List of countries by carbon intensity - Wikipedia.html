This article lists countries by their carbon intensity. Carbon intensity is defined as a country's annual carbon dioxide emissions divided by the country's gross domestic product. National incomes are calculated using market exchange rates (MER) and purchasing power parities (PPP). Units are metric tons of carbon dioxide per thousand year 2005 US dollars.
