Applied Energy

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

Applied Energy is a peer-reviewed academic journal covering research on energy engineering that was established in 1975. It is published by Elsevier and the editor-in-chief is Jinyue Yan. According to the Journal Citation Reports, the journal has a 2014 impact factor of 5.613, ranking it 9th out of 89 journals in the category "Energy & Fuels" and 6th out of 135 journals in "engineering, chemical".[1]
