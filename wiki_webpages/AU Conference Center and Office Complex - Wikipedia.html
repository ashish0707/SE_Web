AU Conference Center and Office Complex

The AU Conference Center and Office Complex (AUCC) is a building in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. It is the headquarters of the African Union. The main building is 99.9 meters tall[1] and it is the tallest building in Addis Ababa. Its cost was US$200 million funded by the Chinese government.[2][3]
