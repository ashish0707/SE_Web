List of geothermal power stations

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

The following page lists all the geothermal fuel power stations that are larger than 50 MW in nameplate capacity which are currently operational or under construction. Those power stations that are smaller than 50 MW, and those that are only at a planning/proposal stage may be found in regional lists, listed at the end of the page.
