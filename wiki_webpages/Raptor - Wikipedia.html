Animals[edit]

  • Raptors, birds that hunt and feed on other animals
  • Velociraptor, a genus of dinosaurs
    • Dromaeosauridae, the family that includes Velociraptor
  • -raptor or raptor-, suffixes used in taxonomy to describe dromeosaurs or similar animals.

Military[edit]

  • Lockheed Martin F-22 Raptor, a United States Air Force stealth fighter aircraft
  • HSM-71, a U.S. Navy helicopter squadron, known by the nickname "The Raptors"
  • VMMT-204, United States Marine Corps Osprey training squadron, known by the nickname "The Raptors"
  • RAPTOR, a reconnaissance pod carried by the RAF Tornado aircraft

Entertainment[edit]

  • Raptor (Cedar Point), an inverted roller coaster at Cedar Point amusement park in Sandusky, Ohio, United States
  • Raptor (Fantasilandia), a roller coaster in Fantasilandia, Chile
  • Raptor (Gardaland), a wing roller coaster at Gardaland amusement park in Italy
  • Raptor: Call of the Shadows, a 1994 vertical scrolling shoot-'em-up video game
  • Raptor convention, a contract bridge convention
  • Raptor (band), a Thai pop duo consisting of two boys, Joni Anwar (also known as Joni Raptor) and Louis Scott
  • A series of electric guitar produced by Peavey Guitars

Fiction[edit]

  • Raptor (Battlestar Galactica), a military spacecraft in the Battlestar Galactica universe
  • Raptor (film), a 2001 made-for-television horror film
  • Raptor (G.I. Joe), a Cobra character in the G.I. Joe universe
  • Raptor (Marvel Comics), the name of three fictional characters in the Marvel Comics universe
  • Raptor (novel), written by Gary Jennings
  • Raptor heavy fighter, a starfighter in the Wing Commander universe
  • Lord Raptor, a character from the Darkstalkers series of fighting games
  • A chapter of the Space Marines in Warhammer 40,000 universe
  • One of the Unreal Tournament 2004 Vehicles (and 2007) as well as a vehicle in Unreal II eXpanded MultiPlayer
  • A character in the XTNCT comic strip

Computers and programming[edit]

  • RAPTOR (software), which provides 3D protein structure prediction for drug discovery researchers
  • Raptor (programming language), visual programming language for developing executable flowcharts
  • WD Raptor, a high-performance hard disk drive from Western Digital
  • Raptor RDF, parser and serializer from the Redland RDF Application Framework
  • Gecko (layout engine), open source layout engine originally released as Raptor
  • Raptor code, a certain type of rateless error correcting code

Vehicles and engines[edit]

  • Independence Raptor, a German paraglider design
  • Raptor (rocket engine), rocket engine being developed by SpaceX for super-heavy lift MCT launch vehicle, for flight after 2020
  • Raptor prototype upper-stage engine, rocket engine prototype for medium-lift Falcon 9/Falcon Heavy-class vehicle, with testing in 2018
  • Raptor 125, a motorbike built by Cagiva
  • Raptor 1000, a motorbike built by Cagiva
  • Raptor 660, an ATV 4-wheel motorcycle produced by Yamaha
  • Ford F-150 Raptor, an off-road utility pickup truck from the Ford F-Series
  • Ford F-150 SVT Raptor, a pickup truck designed to compete in the Baja 1000 off-road race
  • Raptor-class, a design of car ferry used by Red Funnel to cross the Solent
  • Raptor aircraft, an advanced high-speed 5-seat single-engined canard-wing kitbuilt light aircraft
  • Skif Raptor, a Ukrainian paraglider design

Sports[edit]

  • Toronto Raptors, a National Basketball Association team based in Toronto, Ontario, Canada
  • Raptors 905, a NBA Development League team for the Toronto Raptors in Mississauga, Ontario
  • Ogden Raptors, an American professional baseball team in the Pioneer League
  • A downhill ski course adjacent to Birds of Prey (ski course) in Beaver Creek, Colorado, US
