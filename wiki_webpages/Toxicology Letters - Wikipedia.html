Toxicology Letters is a peer-reviewed scientific journal for the rapid publication of short reports on all aspects of toxicology, especially mechanisms of toxicity. Toxicology Letters is the official journal of Eurotox.[1]

Editors-in-chief are Wolfgang Dekant (Julius-Maximilians-Universität Würzburg, Germany),[1][2] Scott Garrett, University of North Dakota, and Yunbo Li (Edward Via College of Osteopathic Medicine, Virginia).[1][3] James P. Kehrer is editor emeritus (University of Alberta, Canada).[1][4]

According to the Journal Citation Reports, its 2014 impact factor is 3.262, ranking it 21st out of 87 journals in the category Toxicology.[5]
