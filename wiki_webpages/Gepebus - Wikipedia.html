Gépébus is a branding of electric autobuses made by Power Vehicle Innovation for the public transport.

History[edit]

In the 1990s Power Vehicle Innovation was among the first to offer full electric buses, the Oréos 22 and the Oréos 55 (for example the Montmartrobus operated by the RATP in the Montmartre neighbourhood in Paris, France).
Since the beginning of the 2000s, Gépébus is the brand name given to its 100% electric autobuses used in the public transport fleet of some local authorities or private actors.

Products[edit]

  • The Oréos 2X : a 22-places electric minibus with a range of 120 km.
  • The Oréos 4X : a 49-places electric midibus with a range of 120 km.
