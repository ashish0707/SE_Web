Wind power in Morocco

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search
Amogdoul Wind Farm, Essaouira

In 2013, 5,05% of electricity produced in Morocco was coming from wind power.[1] The current installed capacity as of 2014, is 750 MW, with an objective of 2,000 MW by 2020.[2]
