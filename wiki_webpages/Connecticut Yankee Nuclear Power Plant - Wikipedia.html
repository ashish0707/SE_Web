Connecticut Yankee Nuclear Power Plant.

Connecticut Yankee Nuclear Power Plant (CY) was a nuclear power plant located in Haddam Neck, Connecticut, that was commissioned in 1968, ceased electricity production in 1996, and was decommissioned by 2004. The plant had a capacity of 582MW.[1] Demolition of the containment dome was completed the week of July 17, 2006.
