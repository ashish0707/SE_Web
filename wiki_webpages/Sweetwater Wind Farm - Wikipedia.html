Sweetwater Wind Farm

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

Sweetwater phase 1 consists of 25 GE 1.5 megawatt S turbines, Sweetwater phase 2 consists of 61 GE 1.5 megawatt SLE turbines, Sweetwater 3 consists of 90 GE 1.5 megawatt XLE turbines.

Sweetwater stage 4 employs 135 Mitsubishi 1.0 megawatt wind turbines and 46 Siemens Wind Power 2.3 megawatt turbines. Its output is being sold to San Antonio’s CPS Energy under a 20-year purchase agreement.[citation needed]

Sweetwater 5 utilizes 35 Siemens 2.3 MW turbines.
