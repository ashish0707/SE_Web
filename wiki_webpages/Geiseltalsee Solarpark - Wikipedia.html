Geiseltalsee Solarpark

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

Geiseltalsee Solarpark, also known as Geiseltalsee, is a 4 MWp photovoltaic power plant located in Merseburg, Germany. The power plant was constructed by BP Solar using 24,864 BP solar modules. The power station was completed in 2004[1][2]
