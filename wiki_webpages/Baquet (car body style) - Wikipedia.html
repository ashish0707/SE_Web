Baquet (car body style)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search
This article relies largely or entirely upon a single source. Relevant discussion may be found on the talk page. Please help improve this article by introducing citations to additional sources. (August 2015)
ALFA 15 HP 4-seater baquet

Baquet is an early car body style, characterised by having two rows of open seats.[1] Baquets usually were without doors, windscreen, or roof.[1] In the United States it was sometimes referred to as a touring car.[1]
