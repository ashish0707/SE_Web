Alinta Energy is an American-owned[1] gas and electricity retailer with headquarters based in Sydney. It has over 800,000 residential, commercial and industrial energy customers.

In March 2011, due to a deleveraging transaction by the TPG Group, Alinta became Alinta Energy.[2]

Electricity generation[edit]

Alinta Energy's 2500MW electricity generation portfolio includes:[3]

  • Port Hedland Power Station
  • Newman Power Station
  • Pinjarra Power Station
  • Wagerup Power Station
  • Port Augusta Power Station (Northern and Playford, both scheduled to permanently close by March 2018)[4]
  • Braemar Power Station
  • Bairnsdale Power Station
  • Glenbrook Power Station New Zealand
