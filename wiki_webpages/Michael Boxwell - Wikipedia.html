Michael Boxwell (born 1 February 1970) is an eco-technology author who has written the books Owning an Electric Car, Solar Electricity Handbook and Proof of Delivery Buyer's Guide, all published by Greenstream Publishing.[1]

He frequently appears on television[2] and on radio talking about electric cars and co-hosted both the Transport Evolved[3] show and TEN with Nikki Gordon-Bloomfield. Both shows are produced by Little Collie Ltd.[4]

Michael Boxwell lives in Warwickshire, England. He is married with two children.
